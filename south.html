South Tunisia

Tunisia is home to the Chott el Jerid, one of the largest salt flats (or chotts) in the Sahara Desert. This vast salt pan covers an area of approximately 5,000 square kilometers (1,900 square miles) and is known for its surreal, otherworldly landscape. During the dry season, the salt flats shimmer under the intense desert sun, creating a mesmerizing sight that stretches to the horizon.

01

The town of Tataouine, located in southern Tunisia, gained international fame as the inspiration for the name of the fictional planet "Tatooine" in the Star Wars saga. George Lucas, the creator of Star Wars, drew inspiration from the unique architecture and desert landscape of Tataouine when naming Luke Skywalker's home planet

02

The Matmata region is renowned for its distinctive troglodyte dwellings, which are traditional underground houses carved into the soft limestone hillsides. These unique homes feature circular rooms with interconnected tunnels and were historically used by the Berber people as protection against the harsh desert climate. Matmata gained global recognition when scenes from the original Star Wars movie were filmed in these underground dwellings.
